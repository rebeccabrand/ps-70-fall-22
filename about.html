Hello! I'm Rebecca, Becca, Bex, etc.

As an interdisciplinary, creative professional, I’m passionate about digital and spatial innovations that improve human relationships with the environment, technology, and one another.

I'm currently pursuing a Masters in Design Engineering at Harvard University’s Graduate School of Design & John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, where I study product design, digital mapping, data visualization, and human-computer interaction through the lenses of social impact and speculative futures.
